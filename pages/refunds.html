Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation

Clients can cancel their project within 24 hours of the initial deposit for a full refund. After 24 hours, a processing fee of 10% will be deducted.

2. Refunds

Once the design phase has been approved by the client, the deposit becomes non-refundable. If ScaleX fails to deliver the project as per the agreed timeline, a partial refund may be issued.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed via Razorpay within 7-10 working days to the original payment method.
